Panther P6C - Taximeter With LCD Display

Relying upon the technological values of many years, SEMITRON's Research and Development team designed the Panther taximeter, equipped with an LCD display. It's a small size taximeter, which aims to meet all the needs of the professional driver, offering the usability of the Panther line and SEMITRON's unsurpassed reliability. Its basic advantages are the smart size, as well as its modern and distinct appearance.

Features
  • Installation in any type of vehicle
  • Universal mounting for any type of vehicle
  • Reinstallation in a new vehicle
  • Multiple fares with pre-programming option
  • Connection with POS terminals, printers and variety of peripheral devices
  • Approved in accordance to the EU standards 2004/22 CE MID
  • Fiscal applications
  • Available in blue and red lighting
